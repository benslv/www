This is going to be a very rough and ready post but it's something that I've been noticing about myself recently that I thought was just about worth writing about.

To cut to the chase, I now find myself trying to figure out if something's been written by generative AI before *actually* committing to reading it properly

I'm quite a fan of skimming through websites like [Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com) and [Lobsters](https://lobste.rs) to find stuff to read when I'm online
